# Abstraction

## Definition

An abstraction is a carefully chosen view of reality that keeps only the information and behavior necessary for the current problem while intentionally ignoring everything else.

---

## Why Abstractions Exist

Reality is too complex.

Software cannot represent everything.

Abstractions reduce complexity by focusing only on what matters.

---

## Examples

Variable

Represents one piece of state.

---

Collection

Represents many related pieces of state.

---

Customer

Represents a business entity.

---

Playlist

Represents a collection of songs.

---

AI Agent

Represents an autonomous decision-making entity.

---

## Engineering Lesson

Model reality first.

Choose abstractions second.

Choose implementation third.

---

## Questions To Ask

What exists in reality?

What matters?

What can be ignored?

Who owns this responsibility?

What abstraction best represents it?
