# Collections

## Why was it invented?

Real-world systems contain groups of related information.

One variable cannot model them effectively.

---

## Problem

Represent multiple related values.

---

## Abstraction

A collection models many related pieces of state.

---

## Reality Examples

Spotify Playlist

Amazon Orders

WhatsApp Messages

YouTube Watch History

---

## Engineering Lesson

Model reality first.

If reality contains many related entities,

a collection is usually the correct abstraction.
