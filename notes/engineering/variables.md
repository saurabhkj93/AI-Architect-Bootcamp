# Variables

## Why was it invented?

Software needed a way to remember one piece of information.

---

## Problem

Represent one changing piece of state.

---

## Abstraction

A variable represents one piece of state.

---

## Reality Examples

- Customer Name
- Temperature
- Hospital Name
- Current User

---

## Engineering Lesson

One variable should represent one meaningful piece of information.

Variables model state.
